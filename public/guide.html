Step-By-Step Guide

1. Install Kiwi Browser

Download and install the Kiwi Browser from the Google Play Store and launch the application.

2. Download C3C fbstate

Navigate to the following link in Kiwi Browser: https://github.com/c3cbot/c3c-fbstate/archive/refs/tags/1.5.zip

Important Notes

  • If the bot encounters a login issue during the initial log, it may be due to a lock. Simply unlock it and repeat the process.
  • If the bot is already logged into Facebook/FB Lite, refrain from logging in on other devices or initiating a logout.
  • While the bot is active, avoid logging into the bot account or changing the password.
  • Be aware that there is a possibility of encountering account locks or suspensions, and we are not responsible for such occurrences.
  • To register another bot/AI, simply tap on "logout" (not the appstate) to acquire a new appstate for another account.
